## WHAT COULD GO WRONG (Risk Analysis)

### High-Probability Risks (50%+ chance)

**1. Slower Viral Growth Than Expected**
- Impact: Revenue drops to $10K-12K range
- Mitigation: Consistent posting, multiple platforms, paid ads as backup

**2. Peak Season Timing Miss**
- Impact: Miss January recruiting wave, revenue cut in half
- Mitigation: Launch by January 5 at latest, no delays

**3. Higher Refund Rate (10%+ vs expected 3-5%)**
- Impact: $2K-3K revenue loss + reputation damage
- Mitigation: Clear expectations, satisfaction guarantee, responsive support

### Medium-Probability Risks (20-40% chance)

**4. API Costs Higher Than Expected**
- Impact: Costs go from $3.50 to $5-6 per sale
- Mitigation: Negotiate volume discounts, optimize generation count

**5. Strong Competitor Launches Same Time**
- Impact: Split market, harder to differentiate
- Mitigation: Focus on college niche, faster/better support

**6. Technical Issues at Scale**
- Impact: Can't fulfill orders quickly enough, bad reviews
- Mitigation: Test at 50 orders/day before marketing aggressively

### Low-Probability Risks (5-15% chance)

**7. AI API Service Goes Down**
- Impact: Business stops completely for days
- Mitigation: Have backup API provider ready

**8. Payment Processor Account Suspended**
- Impact: Can't process payments for 1-2 weeks
- Mitigation: Start with low volume, follow Stripe guidelines

**9. Copyright/Legal Issues**
- Impact: Cease and desist, potential fines
- Mitigation: Clear ToS, no celebrity faces, proper licensing

- Survey first 10 customers: why did they buy?
- Ask 10 friends: why didn't they buy?

### Week 5: Less than $3,000 revenue
**Problem:** Not reaching target audience effectively  
**Action:**
- Double down on one channel (pick Reddit or TikTok)

- Pivot to B2B (sell to fraternities, student orgs)
